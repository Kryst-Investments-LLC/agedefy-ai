# Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short:

- Be respectful, inclusive, and considerate.
- Assume good intent; offer constructive feedback.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind.
- Health-data handling carries elevated trust — never share PHI in
  issues, PRs, or chat.

## Reporting

Email **conduct@agedefy.ai** (PGP key in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)).
Reports are handled confidentially. Maintainers may issue warnings,
temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity.
